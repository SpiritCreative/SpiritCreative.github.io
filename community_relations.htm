Community Relations

NextBridge will engage all stakeholders in any project it develops through honest regular and open communication, seeking and respecting each party's input. We are committed to timely and meaningful dialogue with all stakeholders, including, governments, regulators, and landowners, and believe that this input will be critical to a successful project that seeks to address the needs of everyone involved.

Aboriginal Relations

NextBridge Infrastructure considers the participation of Aboriginal communities to be an essential component of successful transmission projects in Northern Ontario. The partnership is committed to working with Aboriginal communities in Ontario to provide sustainable benefits to those communities.
